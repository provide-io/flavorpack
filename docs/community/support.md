## Before Asking

1. **Search existing discussions and issues**
2. **Check the documentation**
3. **Try the troubleshooting guide**
4. **Prepare a minimal reproduction**

## How to Ask Good Questions

Include:

- FlavorPack version (`flavor --version`)
- Operating system and architecture
- What you're trying to do
- What you expected
- What actually happened
- Error messages (full text)
- Minimal reproduction steps

## Response Times

- **Discussions**: Usually within 24-48 hours
- **Issues**: Triaged within 1 week
- **Security issues**: See security policy

## Community Guidelines

- Be respectful and inclusive
- Stay on topic
- Search before posting
- Provide context and details
- Give back by helping others
